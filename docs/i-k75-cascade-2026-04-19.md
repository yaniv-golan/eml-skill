# i K=75 Cascade (2026-04-19)

Re-derived the standalone `i` witness from the published 9-stage K=91 construction down to K=75 by substituting the identity `i = sqrt(neg(1)) = exp(½·ln(-1)) = exp(iπ/2) = i`, using the K=59 `sqrt` witness and the K=17 beam-discovered `neg` witness. The 16-token savings then cascades into every witness whose stored tree embeds the i subtree (sin, cos, tan, asin, acos, atan, and the three complex-box-honest inverse-trig variants).

## K-composition arithmetic

Substitution cost is `K(W(i_new)) = K(W_shape) + n_i · (K_new − 1)` where `n_i` is the number of i-subtree occurrences in the parent tree. Replacing `K_old = 91` with `K_new = 75` yields a per-occurrence delta of `−16`; total witness delta is `−16 · n_i`.

## Methodology

1. **Construct the new i tree.** Load `sqrt` and `neg` trees from `WITNESSES`. Compose as `sqrt(neg(1))` by string-substituting `x → 1` in `neg.tree` (yields K=17 `neg_of_1`), then substituting `x → neg_of_1` in `sqrt.tree`. Measured `k_tokens(parse(new_tree)) == 75`, `depth == 23`.
2. **Verify i numerically.** `equivalence_check(ast, "i", samples=4096, tolerance=1e-10, domain="complex-box", binary=False)` returned `passed=True, max_abs_diff=2.74e-16` — well under 1e-14.
3. **Scan downstream witnesses.** For every `w.tree` with `w.tree is not None`, counted occurrences of the literal old K=91 i tree string. Found 26 total occurrences across 10 witnesses (i + 9 downstream).
4. **Substitute in place.** Global `src.replace(OLD_I_TREE, NEW_I_TREE)` on `witnesses.py` (26 → 0 occurrences). Updated the `K=` and `depth=` fields for the 10 affected entries plus their notes citing this cascade. Preserved `paper_k`, `paper_k_source`, `paper_k_direct`, `paper_k_direct_lower`, `verdict`, and `proof_url` unchanged — only K/depth/tree/note mutated.
5. **Verify each downstream.** Re-ran `equivalence_check` at `samples=4096, tolerance=1e-10` on each witness's declared domain. All 10 passed. For the three `_complex_box` variants, also re-ran on `complex-box` domain with branch probes active — all 8/8 branch probes passed under tolerance for each.
6. **Update test snapshots.** `_APPEND_ONLY_SNAPSHOT`, `HARVESTED`, `ITER4_HARVESTED`, and `COMPLEX_BOX_INVERSE_TRIG` pin tables in `tests/test_witnesses.py` all updated — these pin K, which is a fact about the stored tree, not a provenance commitment. Two K-assertions in `test_compile.py` and `test_lab_cli.py` (for `sin`) also updated.
7. **Regenerate leaderboard.** `python3 skills/eml-optimize/scripts/leaderboard.py --out docs/leaderboard.md --format md`.

## Why this wasn't done earlier

The published 9-stage K=91 construction for i predates the beam-discovered K=17 `neg` witness (iter-5). Once `neg` was available, the identity `i = sqrt(neg(1))` became strictly shorter than the 9-stage path, but the substitution required touching every downstream witness — which this cascade now does.
